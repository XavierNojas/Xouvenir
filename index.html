CEO / FILMMAKER / PHOTOGRAPHER
Xavier Duane C. Nojas
"The biggest come from the smallest."

Kind of like a flower growing from a tiny seed. This is not only a good philosophy that I live my life by, but this is what I keep in mind when capturing moments. From a small shot of a couple's to the tears flowing from the groom’s eyes when as he sees the bride walking down the isle, every little detail in an event is what makes a beautiful and meaningful moment. My name is Xavier Nojas, the brain and heart of the Xouvenir. I am a chicken wings addict, photographer, and filmmaker. My style of shooting is cinematic, grand, yet emotional and lively. I like to shoot in a way that my shots look like it can be paintings in a museum.
My love of capturing moments and making them extra spicy (like my chicken wings) is what got me into wedding photography and videography. The best thing about being a wedding photographer or videographer is that I get to meet a lot of interesting people, know their story, and tell their story with a touch of my art direction.
Every time I come home from a shoot and go through my shots whether it is a wedding or debut, I always felt like I had something that I was always looking for. My own creativity, a way to express myself. As much as I shoot and immortalize promises of everlasting love, so do I impart taking my own commitment to my clients.
